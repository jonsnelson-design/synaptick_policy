Privacy Policy for SynapTICK

Last updated: March 29, 2026

SynapTICK ("the app") is developed and maintained by SynapTICK. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information You Provide

The app offers an optional field during the welcome screen where you may enter your first name. This name is used solely to personalize in-app greetings (for example, "Good morning, Jon"). This information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted, shared, or accessible by anyone other than you. Providing your name is entirely optional and the app functions fully without it.

Information We Do Not Collect

SynapTICK does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No location data is collected or accessed
  • No usage analytics or crash reports are collected
  • No data of any kind is sent to any server, third party, or developer
  • No account creation is required
  • No advertising identifiers are used

All alarm, reminder, timer, and settings data you create in the app is stored locally on your device only and never leaves it.

Calendar Access

If you choose to enable Google Calendar sync for reminders, the app writes reminder events to your device's local calendar using Android's built-in calendar system. The app does not read your existing calendar data beyond confirming where to write new entries. No calendar data is transmitted to SynapTICK or any third party.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions, each serving a specific function:

  • Notifications — to display alarm and reminder alerts
  • Exact Alarms — to ensure alarms and reminders fire at precisely the scheduled time
  • Display Over Other Apps — to show the alarm fire screen over the lock screen and other apps
  • Battery Optimization Exemption — to prevent the operating system from interrupting scheduled alarms in the background
  • Calendar (Read & Write) — used only if you enable the optional Google Calendar sync feature for reminders

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit data.

Children's Privacy

SynapTICK is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy
